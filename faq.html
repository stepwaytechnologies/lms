Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our courses, enrollment process, and learning experience.

Common Questions

Browse through our FAQs to find quick answers to your questions. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What courses does Stepway Technologies offer?

Stepway Technologies offers a comprehensive range of technology courses including Web Development, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Mobile App Development, and Digital Marketing. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest industry trends and technologies.

Do I need prior experience to enroll in these courses?

No prior experience is required for most of our beginner-level courses. We offer courses for all skill levels - from absolute beginners to advanced professionals looking to upgrade their skills. Each course description clearly indicates the prerequisites, if any.

What is the duration of the courses?

Course durations vary depending on the subject and depth of content. Most courses range from 4 to 12 weeks. We offer both full-time intensive programs and part-time flexible schedules to accommodate different learning needs and availability.

Are the courses self-paced or instructor-led?

We offer both options to cater to different learning preferences. Most courses are instructor-led with live sessions, while we also provide self-paced options with recorded lectures and mentor support. You can choose the format that works best for you during enrollment.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide industry-recognized certificates upon successful completion of all course requirements. Our certificates are widely accepted by employers and can be verified online through our platform. Some advanced courses also offer preparation for industry certifications.

What kind of support do you offer during the course?

We provide comprehensive support throughout your learning journey. This includes direct access to instructors during live sessions, dedicated mentor support, peer discussion forums, and technical assistance. Our support team is available to help with any platform or learning-related issues.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we offer career services to help our graduates transition into tech roles. This includes resume building, interview preparation, portfolio development, and connections to our hiring partners. Many of our courses also include career-focused modules to prepare you for the job market.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. We also offer installment plans for most courses and occasionally provide scholarships or financial aid for eligible students. Please contact our admissions team for more information about payment options.
