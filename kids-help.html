Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my first coding project?
Starting your first project is easy! Go to the "Projects" section and choose "Beginner Projects". We recommend starting with "Create Your First Game" which will guide you step-by-step through creating a simple but fun game.
What should I do if I get stuck on a lesson?
Don't worry! Everyone gets stuck sometimes. You can: 1) Use the "Hint" button for clues, 2) Watch the video explanation again, 3) Ask for help in the community forum, or 4) Contact our support team who are always happy to help!
How do I earn badges and XP?
You earn XP by completing lessons, solving puzzles, and finishing projects. Badges are special rewards for reaching milestones like completing your first project, helping other coders, or mastering certain skills. Keep learning and watch your collection grow!
Can I code on a tablet or phone?
Yes! KidCoder works on tablets and most smartphones. While we recommend using a computer for the best experience, you can complete most lessons and view your progress on mobile devices. Download our app from the App Store or Google Play for the best mobile experience.
How do I change my avatar or username?
You can customize your profile in the "Settings" section. Click on your current avatar to choose a new one, or type a new name in the profile settings. Remember to click "Save Changes" when you're done!
